About Us

Hospital.com is an Healthcare that is built, staffed, and eqipped for the diagnosis of disease; for the treatment, both medicle and surgical, of the sick and the injured; and for their housing during this process.The Hospital.com also serves a center for investigation and for teaching. To better serve the wide-ranging needs of community, the Hospital.com has often developed outpatient facilities, as well as emergency, psychiatric, and rehabilitation services.In addition, "bedless hospitals" provide strictly ambulatory care and day surgery.Patients arrive at the facility for short appointments. They may also stay for treatment in surgical or medical units for part of the day.
